First, you need to identify that we are looking for a right area. This is because large values of ZZ satisfy Z>−0.87Z>-0.87.


By using the z-table, we find the left area (even though we eventually want the right area).

P(Z<−0.87)=0.1922P(Z<-0.87)=0.1922

We use the rule of compliments to determine the right area.

P(Z>−0.87)=1−P(Z<−0.87)=1−0.1922=0.8078\begin{aligned} P(Z > -0.87) &= 1-P(Z < -0.87) \\ &= 1-0.1922 \\ &= 0.8078 \end{aligned}

Method 2: You might also recognize that the normal distribution is symmetric. Thus,

P(Z>−0.87)=P(Z<0.87)=0.8078\begin{aligned} P(Z > -0.87) &= P(Z < 0.87) \\ &= 0.8078 \end{aligned}

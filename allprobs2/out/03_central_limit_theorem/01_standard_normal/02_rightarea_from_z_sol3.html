First, you need to identify that we are looking for a right area. This is because large values of ZZ satisfy Z>−0.94Z>-0.94.


By using the z-table, we find the left area (even though we eventually want the right area).

P(Z<−0.94)=0.1736P(Z<-0.94)=0.1736

We use the rule of compliments to determine the right area.

P(Z>−0.94)=1−P(Z<−0.94)=1−0.1736=0.8264\begin{aligned} P(Z > -0.94) &= 1-P(Z < -0.94) \\ &= 1-0.1736 \\ &= 0.8264 \end{aligned}

Method 2: You might also recognize that the normal distribution is symmetric. Thus,

P(Z>−0.94)=P(Z<0.94)=0.8264\begin{aligned} P(Z > -0.94) &= P(Z < 0.94) \\ &= 0.8264 \end{aligned}

First, draw a sketch. We can label the KK axis by adding integer multiples of 18 to 79. We know to shade the right because high values of KK satisfy the condition K>kK>k (regardless of the exact value of kk). We don’t know exactly where to place the boundary, but we know the right area is 0.81.


We know how to find the left area. P(K<k)=1−P(K>k)=1−0.81=0.19\begin{aligned} P(K<k) &= 1-P(K>k) \\ &= 1-0.81 \\ &= 0.19 \end{aligned}

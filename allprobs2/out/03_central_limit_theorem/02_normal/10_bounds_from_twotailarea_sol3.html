As an intermediate step, let’s find zz such that P(|Z|>z)=0.4P(|Z|>z)=0.4. First, we need to evaluate P(Z<z)P(Z<z).

P(Z<z)=2−P(|Z|>z)2=2−0.42=0.8\begin{aligned} P(Z<z) &= \frac{2-P(|Z|>z)}{2} \\ \\ &= \frac{2-0.4}{2} \\ \\ &= 0.8 \end{aligned}

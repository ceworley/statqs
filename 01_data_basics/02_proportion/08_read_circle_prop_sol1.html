The first 4 questions involve adding up relative frequencies of the indicated intervals. The last 4 questions can be done by guessing and checking until something works.


  1. (ANS=0.6) The proportion of measurements less than 4.5 is 0.6. \[\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}<4.5]=0.6\]
  2. (ANS=0.85) The proportion of measurements more than 2.5 is 0.85. \[\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}>2.5]=0.85\]
  3. (ANS=0.6) The proportion of measurements closer than 1.5 units from 4 is 0.6. \[\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-4|<1.5]=0.6\]
  4. (ANS=0.8) The proportion of measurements farther than 0.5 units from 3 is 0.8. \[\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-3|>0.5]=0.8\]
  5. (ANS=3.5) The proportion of measurements less than 3.5 is 0.35. \[\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}<3.5]=0.35\]
  6. (ANS=3.5) The proportion of measurements more than 3.5 is 0.65. \[\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}>3.5]=0.65\]
  7. (ANS=1.5) The proportion of measurements closer than 1.5 units from 4 is 0.6. \[\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-4|<1.5]=0.6\]
  8. (ANS=2) The proportion of measurements farther than 2 units from 3.5 is 0.35. \[\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-3.5|>2]=0.35\]

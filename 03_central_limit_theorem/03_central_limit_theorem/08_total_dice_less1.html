A fair 4-sided die (with sides numbered 1 through 4) will be rolled 256 times, and the sum (total) of those rolls will be recorded. What is the probability that the sum is less than 634.5? \[P\left(\sum X < 634.5\right) ~=~ ? \]

To help you along, I will calculate the mean and standard deviation of single rolls. The formulas for an \(N\)-sided die can be derived from the formulas for discrete uniform distribution. \[\mu_{ X} ~=~ \frac{N+1}{2} ~=~ \frac{4+1}{2} ~=~ 2.5 \] \[\sigma_{ X} ~=~ \sqrt{\frac{N^2-1}{12}} ~=~ \sqrt{\frac{4^2-1}{12}} ~=~ 1.118034\]

Please use a normal approximation based on the central limit theorem.

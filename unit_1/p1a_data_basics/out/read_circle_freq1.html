  1. Question

    A standard eight-sided die was rolled 100 times, and the results were organized into the pie chart shown below.



    1. Evaluate #[ğ±<7.5]\#[\mathbf{x}<7.5].
    2. Evaluate #[ğ±>2.5]\#[\mathbf{x}>2.5].
    3. Evaluate #[|ğ±âˆ’3|<1.5]\#[|\mathbf{x}-3|<1.5].
    4. Evaluate #[|ğ±âˆ’3|>1.5]\#[|\mathbf{x}-3|>1.5].
    5. Evaluate half-integer boundary bb such that #[ğ±<b]=49\#[\mathbf{x}<b] = 49.
    6. Evaluate half-integer boundary bb such that #[ğ±>b]=51\#[\mathbf{x}>b] = 51.
    7. Evaluate half-integer radius rr such that #[|ğ±âˆ’4|<r]=53\#[|\mathbf{x}-4|<r] = 53.
    8. Evaluate half-integer radius rr such that #[|ğ±âˆ’5|>r]=66\#[|\mathbf{x}-5|>r] = 66.

    Solution

    The first 4 questions involve adding up frequencies of the indicated intervals. The last 4 questions can be done by guessing and checking until something works.


    1. There are 84 measurements less than 7.5. So, #[ğ±<7.5]=84\#[\mathbf{x}<7.5]=84.
    2. There are 73 measurements more than 2.5. So, #[ğ±>2.5]=73\#[\mathbf{x}>2.5]=73.
    3. There are 30 measurements closer than 1.5 units from 3. So, #[|ğ±âˆ’3|<1.5]=30\#[|\mathbf{x}-3|<1.5]=30.
    4. There are 70 measurements further than 1.5 units from 3. So, #[|ğ±âˆ’3|>1.5]=70\#[|\mathbf{x}-3|>1.5]=70.
    5. A boundary at b=4.5b=4.5 has 49 measurements less than it. So, #[ğ±<4.5]=49\#[\mathbf{x}<4.5]=49.
    6. A boundary at b=4.5b=4.5 has 51 measurements more than it. So, #[ğ±>4.5]=51\#[\mathbf{x}>4.5]=51.
    7. An interval with radius r=2.5r=2.5 around center 4 contains 53 measurements. So, #[|ğ±âˆ’4|<2.5]=53\#[|\mathbf{x}-4|<2.5]=53.
    8. An interval with radius r=1.5r=1.5 around center 5 excludes 66 measurements. So, #[|ğ±âˆ’5|>1.5]=66\#[|\mathbf{x}-5|>1.5]=66.

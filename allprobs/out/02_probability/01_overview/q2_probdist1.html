  1. What is the probability of spinning 18? In other words, what is \(\mathbb{P}[X=18]\)?
  2. What is the probability of spinning 10 or 18? In other words, what is \(\mathbb{P}[X=10 ~\text{ or }~ X=18]\)?
  3. If spinning twice, what is the probability of first spinning 10 and then spinning 18? In other words, what is \(\mathbb{P}[X_1=10 ~\text{ and }~ X_2=18]\)?
  4. What is the probability of spinning at most 11? In other words, what is \(\mathbb{P}[X\le 11]\)?
  5. Determine the mean of the probability distribution by using \(\mu = \sum x\cdot \mathbb{P}[x]\).
  6. Determine the standard deviation of the probability distribution by using \(\sigma = \sqrt{\sum (x-\mu)^2 \cdot \mathbb{P}[x]}\).

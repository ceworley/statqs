Do some algebra. \[\text{ME} = \frac{2s}{\sqrt{n}} \] Multiply both sides by \(\sqrt{n}\). \[\text{ME}\sqrt{n} = 2s \] Divide both sides by \(\text{ME}\). \[\sqrt{n} = \frac{2s}{\text{ME}} \] Square both sides. \[n = \left(\frac{2s}{\text{ME}}\right)^2\] Plug in numbers. \[n = \left(\frac{(2)(210)}{2.8}\right)^2\] Evaluate. \[n = 22500\]

The tolerance is \(\pm 1000\), so you can round to 2 significant digits, giving \(n=22000\).

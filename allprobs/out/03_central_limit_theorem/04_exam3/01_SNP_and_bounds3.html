The following questions use \(Z\) to refer to the standard normal variable. You will determine some probabilities and some boundaries.


  1. Determine \(P(Z>0.86)\)
  2. Determine \(z\) such that \(P(Z<z)=0.33\)
  3. Determine \(P(-0.52<Z<0.52)\)
  4. Determine \(P(Z<-0.96)\)
  5. Determine \(z\) such that \(P(Z>z)=0.3974\)

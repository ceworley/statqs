You need to sum the values (\(\sum \mathbf{x}\), see summation) and divide by the sample size (\(n\)).

\[\bar{x} = \frac{\sum \mathbf{x}}{n} = \frac{852.39}{13} = 65.5685\] You can round \(\bar{x}\) to the nearest tenth: 65.6.


In a spreadsheet, you can use the AVERAGE function. You can see a solution spreadsheet here.

Depending on the type of distribution, we can make a strong claim regarding the mean and median.

  • Symmetric (bell, uniform, bimodal)
    • \(\text{mean} \approx \text{median}\)
  • Skew-right
    • \(\text{mean} > \text{median}\)
  • Skew-left
    • \(\text{mean} < \text{median}\)

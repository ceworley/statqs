The first 4 questions involve adding up relative frequencies of the indicated intervals. The last 4 questions can be done by guessing and checking until something works.


  1. (ANS=0.5) The proportion of measurements less than 4.5 is 0.5. \[\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}<4.5]=0.5\]
  2. (ANS=0.8) The proportion of measurements more than 2.5 is 0.8. \[\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}>2.5]=0.8\]
  3. (ANS=0.45) The proportion of measurements closer than 1.5 units from 3 is 0.45. \[\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-3|<1.5]=0.45\]
  4. (ANS=0.75) The proportion of measurements farther than 1 units from 2.5 is 0.75. \[\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-2.5|>1]=0.75\]
  5. (ANS=5.5) The proportion of measurements less than 5.5 is 0.75. \[\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}<5.5]=0.75\]
  6. (ANS=2.5) The proportion of measurements more than 2.5 is 0.8. \[\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}>2.5]=0.8\]
  7. (ANS=0.5) The proportion of measurements closer than 0.5 units from 4 is 0.2. \[\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-4|<0.5]=0.2\]
  8. (ANS=0.5) The proportion of measurements farther than 0.5 units from 4 is 0.8. \[\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-4|>0.5]=0.8\]

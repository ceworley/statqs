The first 4 questions involve adding up relative frequencies of the indicated intervals. The last 4 questions can be done by guessing and checking until something works.


  1. (ANS=0.3) The proportion of measurements less than 2.5 is 0.3. \[\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}<2.5]=0.3\]
  2. (ANS=0.78) The proportion of measurements more than 1.5 is 0.78. \[\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}>1.5]=0.78\]
  3. (ANS=0.14) The proportion of measurements closer than 0.5 units from 4 is 0.14. \[\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-4|<0.5]=0.14\]
  4. (ANS=0.6) The proportion of measurements farther than 1 units from 3.5 is 0.6. \[\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-3.5|>1]=0.6\]
  5. (ANS=4.5) The proportion of measurements less than 4.5 is 0.7. \[\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}<4.5]=0.7\]
  6. (ANS=3.5) The proportion of measurements more than 3.5 is 0.44. \[\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}>3.5]=0.44\]
  7. (ANS=1) The proportion of measurements closer than 1 units from 4.5 is 0.24. \[\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-4.5|<1]=0.24\]
  8. (ANS=1) The proportion of measurements farther than 1 units from 4.5 is 0.76. \[\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-4.5|>1]=0.76\]

  1. (ANS=0) The proportion of measurements between 51.5 and 52.5 is 0. \[\text{prop}[51.5<\mathbf{x}<52.5]=0\]
  2. (ANS=0.8) The proportion of measurements outside 51.5 and 53.5 is 0.8. \[\text{prop}[\texttt{NOT}(51.5<\mathbf{x}<53.5)]=0.8\]
  3. (ANS=0.1) The proportion of measurements closer than 0.5 cm from 55 cm is 0.1. \[\text{prop}\left[\big|\mathbf{x}-55\big|<0.5\right] = 0.1\]
  4. (ANS=0.4) The proportion of measurements farther than 2 cm from 54.5 cm is 0.4. \[\text{prop}\left[\big|\mathbf{x}-54.5\big|>2\right] = 0.4\]
  5. (ANS=0.5) You can guess and check until you find a half-integer that satisfies the equation. It turns out that \(r = 0.5\) satisfies the equation, because \[\text{prop}\left[\big|\mathbf{x}-54\big|<0.5\right] ~=~ 0.1\]
  6. (ANS=3.5) You can guess and check until you find a half-integer that satisfies the equation. It turns out that \(r = 3.5\) satisfies the equation, because \[\text{prop}\left[\big|\mathbf{x}-54\big|>3.5\right] ~=~ 0.2\]

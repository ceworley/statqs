  1. (ANS=0.1) The proportion of measurements between 55.5 and 56.5 is 0.1. \[\text{prop}[55.5<\mathbf{x}<56.5]=0.1\]
  2. (ANS=0.7) The proportion of measurements outside 52.5 and 55.5 is 0.7. \[\text{prop}[\texttt{NOT}(52.5<\mathbf{x}<55.5)]=0.7\]
  3. (ANS=0.3) The proportion of measurements closer than 1 cm from 57.5 cm is 0.3. \[\text{prop}\left[\big|\mathbf{x}-57.5\big|<1\right] = 0.3\]
  4. (ANS=1) The proportion of measurements farther than 1 cm from 51.5 cm is 1. \[\text{prop}\left[\big|\mathbf{x}-51.5\big|>1\right] = 1\]
  5. (ANS=1.5) You can guess and check until you find a half-integer that satisfies the equation. It turns out that \(r = 1.5\) satisfies the equation, because \[\text{prop}\left[\big|\mathbf{x}-55\big|<1.5\right] ~=~ 0.4\]
  6. (ANS=2) You can guess and check until you find an integer that satisfies the equation. It turns out that \(r = 2\) satisfies the equation, because \[\text{prop}\left[\big|\mathbf{x}-55.5\big|>2\right] ~=~ 0.4\]

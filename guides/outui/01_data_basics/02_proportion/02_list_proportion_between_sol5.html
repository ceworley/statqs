  1. (ANS=0.4) The proportion of measurements between 50.5 and 54.5 is 0.4. \[\text{prop}[50.5<\mathbf{x}<54.5]=0.4\]
  2. (ANS=0.7) The proportion of measurements outside 56.5 and 59.5 is 0.7. \[\text{prop}[\texttt{NOT}(56.5<\mathbf{x}<59.5)]=0.7\]
  3. (ANS=0.1) The proportion of measurements closer than 0.5 cm from 54 cm is 0.1. \[\text{prop}\left[\big|\mathbf{x}-54\big|<0.5\right] = 0.1\]
  4. (ANS=0.9) The proportion of measurements farther than 0.5 cm from 53 cm is 0.9. \[\text{prop}\left[\big|\mathbf{x}-53\big|>0.5\right] = 0.9\]
  5. (ANS=1) You can guess and check until you find an integer that satisfies the equation. It turns out that \(r = 1\) satisfies the equation, because \[\text{prop}\left[\big|\mathbf{x}-56.5\big|<1\right] ~=~ 0.1\]
  6. (ANS=3.5) You can guess and check until you find a half-integer that satisfies the equation. It turns out that \(r = 3.5\) satisfies the equation, because \[\text{prop}\left[\big|\mathbf{x}-54\big|>3.5\right] ~=~ 0.3\]

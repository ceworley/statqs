  3. (ANS=2) There are 2 measurements closer than 2 units from 35.5. It might help to rephrase the problem as finding how many measurements are between 33.5 and 37.5. These boundaries are found by subtracting 2 from 35.5 and adding 2 to 35.5. \[\#\left[\big|\mathbf{x}-35.5\big|<2\right]=2\]

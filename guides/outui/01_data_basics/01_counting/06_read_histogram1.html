A random procedure generated 120 measurements, which were organized into the histogram shown below. You can assume the measurements are of a continuous variable, such that every measurement is in one of the intervals (and not on a break).



  1. Evaluate \(\#[\mathbf{x}<62.5]\).
  2. Evaluate \(\#[\mathbf{x}>62.5]\).
  3. Evaluate \(\#[|\mathbf{x}-62|<1]\).
  4. Evaluate \(\#[|\mathbf{x}-62.75|>0.25]\).
  5. Find boundary \(b\) such that \(\#[\mathbf{x}<b] = 48\).
  6. Find boundary \(b\) such that \(\#[\mathbf{x}>b] = 104\).
  7. Find radius \(r\) such that \(\#[|\mathbf{x}-63.5|<r] = 52\).
  8. Find radius \(r\) such that \(\#[|\mathbf{x}-63.5|>r] = 104\).

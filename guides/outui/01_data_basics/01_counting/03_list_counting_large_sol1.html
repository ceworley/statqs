  1. (ANS=90) There are 90 measurements.
  2. (ANS=40) There are 40 measurements less than 94.2.
  3. (ANS=47) There are 47 measurements greater than 94.5.
  4. (ANS=33) There are 33 measurements closer than 2 units from 97. You could also say there are 33 measurements inside 95 to 99.
  5. (ANS=74) There are 74 measurements farther than 1 units from 92.3. You could also say there are 74 measurements outside 91.3 to 93.3.

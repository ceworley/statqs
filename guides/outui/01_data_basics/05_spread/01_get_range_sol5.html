To determine the range by hand, you subtract the minimum value from the maximum value. You could first sort all the data. \[\text{sort}(\mathbf{x}) = 21.58, 24.67, 24.89, 26.75, 27.61, 28.09, 28.31, 28.32, 29.37, 30.28, 31.13, 32.79 \] Determine the minimum. \[\text{min}(\mathbf{x}) = 21.58 \] Determine the maximum. \[\text{max}(\mathbf{x}) = 32.79\] Take the difference. \[\text{range}(\mathbf{x}) = \text{max}(\mathbf{x})-\text{min}(\mathbf{x}) = 32.79-21.58 = 11.21 \]

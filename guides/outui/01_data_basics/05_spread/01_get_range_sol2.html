To determine the range by hand, you subtract the minimum value from the maximum value. You could first sort all the data. \[\text{sort}(\mathbf{x}) = 30.00, 30.20, 31.23, 33.32, 33.98, 34.00, 34.82, 34.91, 35.00, 35.00 \] Determine the minimum. \[\text{min}(\mathbf{x}) = 30 \] Determine the maximum. \[\text{max}(\mathbf{x}) = 35\] Take the difference. \[\text{range}(\mathbf{x}) = \text{max}(\mathbf{x})-\text{min}(\mathbf{x}) = 35-30 = 5 \]

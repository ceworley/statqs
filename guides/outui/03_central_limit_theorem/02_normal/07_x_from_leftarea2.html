Random variable \(G\) is normally distributed with mean \(\mu = 330\) and standard deviation \(\sigma = 80\). \[G \sim \text{N}(330,\,80) \] Evaluate \(g\) such that \(P(G<g) = 0.89\). In other words, determine an upper boundary such that a normal spinner with mean 330 and standard deviation 80 lands under that boundary 89% of the time.

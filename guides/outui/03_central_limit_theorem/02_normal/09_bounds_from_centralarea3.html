Random variable \(H\) is normally distributed with mean \(\mu = 6.4\) and standard deviation \(\sigma = 1.8\). \[H \sim \text{N}(6.4,\,1.8) \]


  1. Evaluate \(d\) such that \(P\left(\big|H-6.4\big|<d\right) = 0.1\). In other words, determine the distance from mean of two boundaries such that this normal spinner lands within those boundaries 10% of the time.
  2. Let \(h_1 = 6.4-d\) and \(h_2 = 6.4+d\), such that \(P\left(h_1 < H < h_2\right) = 0.1\). Evaluate \(h_1\).
  3. Using the definitions above, evaluate \(h_2\)

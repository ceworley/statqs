The following questions use \(Z\) to refer to the standard normal variable. You will determine some probabilities and some boundaries.


  1. Determine \(P(-0.24<Z<0.24)\)
  2. Determine \(P(Z>-1.6)\)
  3. Determine \(z\) such that \(P(Z<z)=0.281\)
  4. Determine \(P(Z<-0.56)\)
  5. Determine \(z\) such that \(P(Z>z)=0.4325\)

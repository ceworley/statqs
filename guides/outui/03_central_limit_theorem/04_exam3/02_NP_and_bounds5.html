Let random variable \(X\) be normally distributed with mean \(\mu=30\) and standard deviation \(\sigma=2\).


  1. Determine \(P(28.6<X<31.4)\)
  2. Determine \(P(X<27.4)\)
  3. Determine \(P(X>30.8)\)
  4. Determine \(x\) such that \(P(X>x)=0.5398\)
  5. Determine \(x\) such that \(P(X<x)=0.7881\)

Let random variable \(X\) be normally distributed with mean \(\mu=35\) and standard deviation \(\sigma=2\).


  1. Determine \(P(X<35.2)\)
  2. Determine \(P(X>38.2)\)
  3. Determine \(P(32.2<X<37.8)\)
  4. Determine \(x\) such that \(P(X>x)=0.8643\)
  5. Determine \(x\) such that \(P(X<x)=0.1587\)

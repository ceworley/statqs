Method 1: Determine the area of each tail and the center. Both tails have the same area, and all three areas add to 1. Thus, \[P(Z>z) = \frac{0.64}{2} = 0.32\]

\[P(Z<-z) = \frac{0.64}{2} = 0.32\]

(You can round \(t^\star\) to the nearest hundredth and the boundaries to the nearest tenth.)


  1. Determine the lower boundary of the confidence interval by using \(\bar{x}-\frac{t^\star s}{\sqrt{n}}\).
  2. Determine the upper boundary of the confidence interval by using \(\bar{x}+\frac{t^\star s}{\sqrt{n}}\).

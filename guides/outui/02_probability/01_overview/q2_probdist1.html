  1. What is the probability of spinning 24? In other words, what is \(\mathbb{P}[X=24]\)?
  2. What is the probability of spinning 5 or 15? In other words, what is \(\mathbb{P}[X=5 ~\text{ or }~ X=15]\)?
  3. If spinning twice, what is the probability of first spinning 5 and then spinning 15? In other words, what is \(\mathbb{P}[X_1=5 ~\text{ and }~ X_2=15]\)?
  4. What is the probability of spinning at most 15? In other words, what is \(\mathbb{P}[X\le 15]\)?
  5. Determine the mean of the probability distribution by using \(\mu = \sum x\cdot \mathbb{P}[x]\).
  6. Determine the standard deviation of the probability distribution by using \(\sigma = \sqrt{\sum (x-\mu)^2 \cdot \mathbb{P}[x]}\).

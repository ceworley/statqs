  1. What is the probability of spinning 21? In other words, what is \(\mathbb{P}[X=21]\)?
  2. What is the probability of spinning 11 or 16? In other words, what is \(\mathbb{P}[X=11 ~\text{ or }~ X=16]\)?
  3. If spinning twice, what is the probability of first spinning 11 and then spinning 16? In other words, what is \(\mathbb{P}[X_1=11 ~\text{ and }~ X_2=16]\)?
  4. What is the probability of spinning at least 16? In other words, what is \(\mathbb{P}[X\ge 16]\)?
  5. Determine the mean of the probability distribution by using \(\mu = \sum x\cdot \mathbb{P}[x]\).
  6. Determine the standard deviation of the probability distribution by using \(\sigma = \sqrt{\sum (x-\mu)^2 \cdot \mathbb{P}[x]}\).

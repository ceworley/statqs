Method 1: Determine the area of each tail. Both tails have the same area, and all three areas add to 1. Thus, \[P(Z>z) = \frac{1-0.9}{2} = 0.05\]

\[\begin{aligned} P(Z<z) &= 0.05+0.9 \\ &= 0.95 \end{aligned}\]

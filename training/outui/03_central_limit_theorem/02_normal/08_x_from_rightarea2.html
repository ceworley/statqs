Random variable \(G\) is normally distributed with mean \(\mu = 0.98\) and standard deviation \(\sigma = 0.22\). \[G \sim \text{N}(0.98,\,0.22) \] Evaluate \(g\) such that \(P(G>g) = 0.73\). In other words, determine an lower boundary such that a normal spinner with mean 0.98 and standard deviation 0.22 lands above that boundary 73% of the time.

Random variable \(R\) is normally distributed with mean \(\mu = 74\) and standard deviation \(\sigma = 23\). \[R \sim \text{N}(74,\,23) \] Evaluate \(r\) such that \(P(R<r) = 0.92\). In other words, determine an upper boundary such that a normal spinner with mean 74 and standard deviation 23 lands on a value less than that boundary 92% of the time.

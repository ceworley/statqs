Random variable \(V\) is normally distributed with mean \(\mu = 7.4\) and standard deviation \(\sigma = 1.6\). \[V \sim \text{N}(7.4,\,1.6) \] Evaluate \(v\) such that \(P(V<v) = 0.95\). In other words, determine an upper boundary such that a normal spinner with mean 7.4 and standard deviation 1.6 lands under that boundary 95% of the time.

Random variable \(G\) is normally distributed with mean \(\mu = 85\) and standard deviation \(\sigma = 22\). \[G \sim \text{N}(85,\,22) \]


  1. Evaluate \(d\) such that \(P\left(\big|G-85\big|<d\right) = 0.7\). In other words, determine the distance from mean of two boundaries such that this normal spinner lands within those boundaries 70% of the time.
  2. Let \(g_1 = 85-d\) and \(g_2 = 85+d\), such that \(P\left(g_1 < G < g_2\right) = 0.7\). Evaluate \(g_1\).
  3. Using the definitions above, evaluate \(g_2\)

Random variable \(Q\) is normally distributed with mean \(\mu = 0.1\) and standard deviation \(\sigma = 0.03\). \[Q \sim \text{N}(0.1,\,0.03) \]


  1. Evaluate \(d\) such that \(P\left(\big|Q-0.1\big|<d\right) = 0.88\). In other words, determine the distance from mean of two boundaries such that this normal spinner lands within those boundaries 88% of the time.
  2. Let \(q_1 = 0.1-d\) and \(q_2 = 0.1+d\), such that \(P\left(q_1 < Q < q_2\right) = 0.88\). Evaluate \(q_1\).
  3. Using the definitions above, evaluate \(q_2\)

Random variable \(F\) is normally distributed with mean \(\mu = 0.58\) and standard deviation \(\sigma = 0.15\). \[F \sim \text{N}(0.58,\,0.15) \] Evaluate \(f\) such that \(P(F>f) = 0.81\). In other words, determine an lower boundary such that a normal spinner with mean 0.58 and standard deviation 0.15 lands over that boundary 81% of the time.

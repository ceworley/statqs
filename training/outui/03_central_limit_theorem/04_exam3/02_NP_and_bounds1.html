Let random variable \(X\) be normally distributed with mean \(\mu=35\) and standard deviation \(\sigma=7\).


  1. Determine \(P(X>34.3)\)
  2. Determine \(x\) such that \(P(X>x)=0.6915\)
  3. Determine \(P(X<18.9)\)
  4. Determine \(x\) such that \(P(X<x)=0.3821\)
  5. Determine \(P(32.2<X<37.8)\)

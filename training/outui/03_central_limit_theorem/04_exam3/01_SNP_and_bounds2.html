03_central_limit_theorem/04_exam3/01_SNP_and_bounds_v2

The following questions use \(Z\) to refer to the standard normal variable. You will determine some probabilities and some boundaries.


  1. Determine \(z\) such that \(P(Z<z)=0.3085\)
  2. Determine \(z\) such that \(P(Z>z)=0.1711\)
  3. Determine \(P(-1.4<Z<1.4)\)
  4. Determine \(P(Z<-0.37)\)
  5. Determine \(P(Z>-0.24)\)

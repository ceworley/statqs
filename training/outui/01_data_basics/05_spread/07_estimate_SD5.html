When a symmetric distribution is sampled thoroughly (\(n>100\)), you can use the following rules of thumb to estimate the mean and standard deviation.

Shape Estimated mean Estimated standard deviation
Bell \(\frac{\min(x)+\max(x)}{2}\) \(\frac{\max(x)-\min(x)}{6}\)
Uniform \(\frac{\min(x)+\max(x)}{2}\) \(\frac{\max(x)-\min(x)}{\sqrt{12}}\)
Bimodal \(\frac{\min(x)+\max(x)}{2}\) \(\frac{\max(x)-\min(x)}{2}\)

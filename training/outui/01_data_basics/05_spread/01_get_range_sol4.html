To determine the range by hand, you subtract the minimum value from the maximum value. You could first sort all the data. \[\text{sort}(\mathbf{x}) = 33.11, 33.65, 34.15, 34.54, 34.58, 34.83, 34.89, 34.94 \] Determine the minimum. \[\text{min}(\mathbf{x}) = 33.11 \] Determine the maximum. \[\text{max}(\mathbf{x}) = 34.94\] Take the difference. \[\text{range}(\mathbf{x}) = \text{max}(\mathbf{x})-\text{min}(\mathbf{x}) = 34.94-33.11 = 1.83 \]

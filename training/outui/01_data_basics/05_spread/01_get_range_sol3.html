To determine the range by hand, you subtract the minimum value from the maximum value. You could first sort all the data. \[\text{sort}(\mathbf{x}) = 51.18, 51.62, 52.43, 53.18, 54.07, 54.37, 54.45, 55.54, 56.61, 56.68, 57.33, 59.11, 59.65, 60.09, 61.49 \] Determine the minimum. \[\text{min}(\mathbf{x}) = 51.18 \] Determine the maximum. \[\text{max}(\mathbf{x}) = 61.49\] Take the difference. \[\text{range}(\mathbf{x}) = \text{max}(\mathbf{x})-\text{min}(\mathbf{x}) = 61.49-51.18 = 10.31 \]

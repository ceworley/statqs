Algebra

With a little bit of algebra, we can simplify the formula for \(\text{MAD}_\text{prop}\). (I’ve not shown the algebra…)

\[\text{MAD}_\text{prop} = 2(1-\hat{p})\hat{p}\]

In other words, if \(n_0\) is the number of 0s and \(n_1\) is the number of 1s, then \[\text{MAD}_\text{prop} = 2 \cdot \frac{n_0}{n} \cdot \frac{n_1}{n}\] So, in this case: \[\text{MAD}_\text{prop} = 2 \cdot \frac{5}{8}\cdot \frac{3}{8} = 0.46875\]

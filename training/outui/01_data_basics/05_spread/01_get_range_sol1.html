To determine the range by hand, you subtract the minimum value from the maximum value. You could first sort all the data. \[\text{sort}(\mathbf{x}) = 45.00, 45.02, 45.15, 45.28, 45.44, 45.44, 45.66, 45.86, 46.00, 46.62, 46.72, 48.36, 50.57, 51.45 \] Determine the minimum. \[\text{min}(\mathbf{x}) = 45 \] Determine the maximum. \[\text{max}(\mathbf{x}) = 51.45\] Take the difference. \[\text{range}(\mathbf{x}) = \text{max}(\mathbf{x})-\text{min}(\mathbf{x}) = 51.45-45 = 6.45 \]

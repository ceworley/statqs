You need to sum the values (\(\sum \mathbf{x}\), see summation) and divide by the sample size (\(n\)).

\[\bar{x} = \frac{\sum \mathbf{x}}{n} = \frac{537.85}{9} = 59.7611\] You can round \(\bar{x}\) to the nearest tenth: 59.8.


In a spreadsheet, you can use the AVERAGE function. You can see a solution spreadsheet here.

In R, you can use the MEAN function.

# First, get the csv into the working directory... then...
data = read.csv("get_mean.csv")
x = data$x
xbar = mean(x)
round(xbar,1)

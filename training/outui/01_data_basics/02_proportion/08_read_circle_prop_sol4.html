  4. (ANS=0.81) The proportion of measurements farther than 1 units from 3.5 is 0.81. \[\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-3.5|>1]=0.81\]
  5. (ANS=3.5) The proportion of measurements less than 3.5 is 0.34. \[\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}<3.5]=0.34\]

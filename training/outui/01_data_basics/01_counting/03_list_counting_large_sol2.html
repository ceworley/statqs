  1. (ANS=80) There are 80 measurements.
  2. (ANS=26) There are 26 measurements less than 22.5.
  3. (ANS=11) There are 11 measurements greater than 28.6.
  4. (ANS=68) There are 68 measurements closer than 4.4 units from 24.6. You could also say there are 68 measurements inside 20.2 to 29.
  5. (ANS=47) There are 47 measurements farther than 2.5 units from 27.5. You could also say there are 47 measurements outside 25 to 30.

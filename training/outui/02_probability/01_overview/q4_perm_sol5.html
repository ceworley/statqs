This scenario describes a permutations problem (order matters). We are considering the nonrepeating sequences of size 5 from a set of size 24.

\[\begin{aligned} {_nP_r} &= \frac{n!}{(n-r)!} \\\\ n &= 24 \\\\ r &= 5 \\\\ {_{24}P_{5}} &= \frac{24!}{(24-5)! } \\\\ &= \frac{24!}{19!} \\\\ &= 24\cdot23\cdot22\cdot21\cdot20 \\\\ &= 5100480 \end{aligned}\]

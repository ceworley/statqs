This scenario describes a permutations problem (order matters). We are considering the nonrepeating sequences of size 4 from a set of size 24.

\[\begin{aligned} {_nP_r} &= \frac{n!}{(n-r)!} \\\\ n &= 24 \\\\ r &= 4 \\\\ {_{24}P_{4}} &= \frac{24!}{(24-4)! } \\\\ &= \frac{24!}{20!} \\\\ &= 24\cdot23\cdot22\cdot21 \\\\ &= 255024 \end{aligned}\]

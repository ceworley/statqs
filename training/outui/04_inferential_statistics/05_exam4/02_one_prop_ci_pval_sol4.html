By hand, this is easiest to do with a normal approximation without the continuity correction.

\[n = 119\] \[\hat{p} = 0.2941\] \[z^\star = 1.96 \] \[\text{CI}_\text{boundaries} = \hat{p} \pm z^\star\sqrt{\frac{\hat{p}(1-\hat{p})}{n}} \] \[\text{CI}_\text{boundaries} = (0.2122506,0.3759847)\] \[z = \frac{\hat{p}-p_0}{\sqrt{p_0(1-p_0)/n}} = 2.5667558\]

\[p\text{-value} = P\left(|Z|>2.5667558\right) = 0.0102655\]
